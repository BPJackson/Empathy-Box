WELCOME TO EMPATHY BOX

CREATE EXPERIENCES FROM YOUR PHOTOS

Empathy box is a tool that allows you to capture life in a stereoscopic way. ------------------------------------------------ Stereoscopic you say? It's the way our eyes see depth in the world, and can be recreated with two photos taken from slightly different angles. ------------------------------------------------ That's where you come in. Using your smartphone, you can create a stereoscopic experience from your own photos! ------------------------------------------------ Make sure to photograph a subject between 3 and 20 feet away. ------------------------------------------------ For the best results, make sure your phone is in landscape mode, and take your photos ahead of time.
